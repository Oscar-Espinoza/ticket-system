| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-03-01 | Elevation of Privilege | requireProjectOwner | mitigate | Reuse requireProjectMember (throws before any read for non-members); reject role !== 'owner' with ProjectAccessError. Server-side only seam (D-30). |
| T-03-02 | Tampering | project_member rows under concurrent join | mitigate | Live unique(project_id,user_id) constraint (Task 1 push) — concurrent double-insert raises 23505, the race-safe backstop for D-29. |
| T-03-03 | Spoofing | falsy projectId/userId | mitigate | requireProjectMember already rejects falsy ids (WR-01); requireProjectOwner inherits that guard. |
